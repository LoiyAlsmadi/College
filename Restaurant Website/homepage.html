About


La Toscana is a traditional Italian Restaurant & Pizzaria,
offering wonderfully delicious food at extravagant prices.
Since opening in 2000, La Toscana has won many awards,
including "Best Italian Restuarant 2003".
We are in the heart of Dublin City. Close to OConnell Bridge,
with Dublin hotels and other attractions of Historical
and Shopping interests just a short walk away.
La Toscana has a breathtaking atmosphere
and is a place where you can relax and dine at ease.
With succulent and scrumptious food, La Toscana is the place to eat.
